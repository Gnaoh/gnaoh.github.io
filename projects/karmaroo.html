KarmaRoo

Description

People raise money for disaster relief, they search for missing children, and they bring attention to the issues they care about. We’ve seen from our community that when people take action, lives are changed. So, we’ve created a centralized platform, KarmaRoo, to allow people to donate money to charities they support.

It is a web application that aggregates chartities from all over the world, enabling users to conveniently donate money to multiple foundations at the same time. We hope the convenience will help nonprofits reach new supporters, engage their community and get the valuable funding they need to continue their good work.
