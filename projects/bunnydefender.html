Bunny Defender

Description

In the year 2016, scientists have identified an unexpected meteor shower approaching Bunny Island. Individuals of the community are urged to use their weapons to prevent the catastrophe. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will be to protect, defend, and guarantee the safety of all bunnies. Good luck.

Bunny Defender was a game that I made for the "lighting talk" module at General Assembly. The premise of the module is to pick a technology that you're unfamiliar with, spend a day learning it, and then use it to build an application. The technology I taught myself was Phaser.IO (a desktop and mobile HTML 5 game framework) and the result was this game.
